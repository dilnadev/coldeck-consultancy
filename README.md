# Coldeck Solutions — Official Website

Corporate website for **Coldeck Solutions**, a B2B cold chain consultancy based in India with 20+ years of industry experience. The site presents the firm's services, sectors, founder story, and contact details to prospective clients across pharma, food processing, logistics, retail, and industrial sectors.

---

## Pages

| Route | Description |
|---|---|
| `/` | Home — Hero, Stats, Services overview, Why Choose Us, Sectors, FAQ, CTA |
| `/about` | About — Founder story, Mission & Vision, Company values |
| `/services` | Services — Detailed breakdown of all 6 advisory services |
| `/sectors` | Sectors — 7 industry verticals served |
| `/contact` | Contact — Inquiry form with WhatsApp integration |

---

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router) + TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v3 with custom brand tokens
- **Animations:** Framer Motion v12
- **Fonts:** Manrope (headings) + Inter (body) via `next/font`
- **Deployment:** Vercel
